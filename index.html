What is BJJ?

“It’s as real as it can get. That has made me a better person. It’s made me a better man. It’s made me understand myself, my weaknesses, my strengths, the stuff I need to work on. Jiu-jitsu has been one of the most valuable tools I’ve ever had in my life.” -- Joe Rogan

BJJ is an excellent, inclusive system to build fitness, flexibility and strength - even if you have no interest in fighting, even if you are small, even if you consider yourself weak.
Above all though, it's great fun.
